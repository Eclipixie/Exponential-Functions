## Basic Laws

$$a^m \cdot a^n=a^{m+n}$$

$$a^m/a^n=a^{m-n}$$

$$(a^m)^n=a^{mn}$$

$$(ab)^m=a^mb^m$$

$$a^\frac{p}{q}=(a^p)^\frac{1}{q}=(a^\frac{1}{q})^p$$

When $a\neq0$:

$$a^{-n}=\frac{1}{a^n}$$

$$a^0=1$$
## Non-integer Powers
* $a^\frac{1}{q}=\sqrt[q]{a}$
# Functions
Exponential functions are defined as 

$$y=Aa^{x+b}+c$$
## Transforms
* $\pm c$ moves the graph up and down
* $\pm b$ moves the graph left and right
